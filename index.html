Neothyne is a Free and Open Source game, built on a new engine using SDL2 and OpenGL

  • A focus for old school twitch gameplay.
  • New open source engine built from the ground up focusing on providing modern rendering techniques for both high-end and low-end systems.
  • Available for Windows, GNU/Linux, BSD, OS X and mobile devices.
  • Built-in level editor to create, modify and explore one's artistic abilities.

Getting involved

Neothyne is a by the people for the people project. We encourage people to get involved and shape the direction of this project. To get involved drop by our IRC channel #neothyne on Freenode We welcome your ideas.
